Founder & Lead Therapist

Dr. Phillip Herel, DPT

Dr. Herel earned his Doctorate in Physical Therapy from Stony Brook University in 2014. An upstate New York native, his professional interests center on sports medicine, orthopedics, and neurological conditions.

While at Stony Brook, he contributed to research on trans-magnetic cranial stimulation for central nervous system impairments. His clinical background spans geriatrics, pediatrics, sports, orthopedics, neurology, and home health - using a holistic mind-body healing approach.

His own health journey motivated a shift toward functional and alternative approaches. With support from his wife Monica, he pursued holistic treatments and supplementation, experiencing significant personal improvements that now inform his integrative practice philosophy.

He has expanded services by partnering with CellCore and Fullscript to offer integrative healing protocols that address root causes of illness alongside physical rehabilitation.
