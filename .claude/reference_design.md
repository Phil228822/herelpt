# Reference: Design Decisions

## Color Palette
- Background: #FAF8F6 (warm off-white)
- Alt background: #F5F1ED (warmer neutral)
- Primary: #5B7C6E (muted forest green)
- Accent: #C4A882 (warm sand/tan)
- Text: #3D3D3D (warm brown-gray)
- Muted text: #6B6157
- Dark sections: #2C3530 (deep forest)
- Footer: #1E2723

## Typography
- Headlines: Fustat Bold, letter-spacing -0.03em
- Body: Schibsted Grotesk 400-600
- Nav logo: Schibsted Grotesk 600, 24px, -1.44px tracking
- Logo style: "Herel" in muted gray, "PT" in accent sand color
- Testimonial names: Caveat (handwritten)

## Design Rules (from user feedback)
- NO emoji icons anywhere
- NO Lucide icon boxes on cards (user called them "tacky and AI")
- Typography-only cards are preferred
- Real photos only, no stock placeholders
- Warm, organic feel - not cold/clinical
- Asymmetric grid layouts (1.2fr 1fr)
- Varied section spacing

## Hero (Homepage)
- Video background with parallax (position: fixed)
- Dark translucent nav bar with backdrop blur
- Glassmorphism badge component
- Centered text, Fustat headline
- Two CTA buttons: solid white + ghost outline

## Footer
- Single dark footer across all pages
- 4-column grid: brand, pages, resources, contact
- No cactus image (was removed)
- Consistent font sizing (0.88rem for links)
