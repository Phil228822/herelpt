Why Choose Us

Experience physical therapy the way it should be.

One-on-One Care

One-on-one treatment with a Doctor of Physical Therapy for the full duration of every appointment. No aides, assistants, or ineffective modalities. Your session is entirely focused on you.

Individualized Treatment

Personalized and effective treatment plans tailored to your specific injury, lifestyle, and goals. A "one size fits all" approach doesn't work in physical therapy - and we never use one.

Modern Facility

A clean, comfortable, and open atmosphere. Private gym, treatment rooms, and pilates studio. Handicap accessible with plenty of free parking right off El Paseo.

Accommodating Hours

Appointments available in the early morning and later in the evening to accommodate your schedule. We work around your life, not the other way around.
